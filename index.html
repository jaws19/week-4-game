CRYSTALS COLLECTOR

GAME RULES

You will be given a Target Score at the start of the game.

Clicking on any of the four crystals you will add its hidden value to Your Score.

You win the game by matching Your Score to the Target Score. You lose the game if Your Score goes above the Target Score.

After each win or loss the game will change the hidden values of each crystal.

Good luck!
